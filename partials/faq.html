NEW HUSKIES FAQ

Q: How do I find my classes on campus?

A: Use the interactive map in the plaza! All buildings have letter codes (like DOU for Dougan). Look for colored signs with building letters at every entrance.

Q: Where can I get my student ID?

A: Visit the Husky Card office in Mattress Factory (MF) building. Bring photo ID! Pro tip: Your ID gives you free bus rides in Pierce County.

Q: How does parking work?

A: Get parking permits online! Yellow zones = student parking. Download the "PayByPhone" app for hourly parking. Better yet - use free transport system!

Q: What's the best food on campus?

A: Dawg House in Dougan (DOU) has killer hotdogs! Or, get a free quick meal at the cupboard.

Q: How do I join student clubs?

A: Go to the Dawg House during Week 1! You will meet many new students and clubs there. Can't find a club? Start your own with 5 people!

Q: Where do I get textbooks?

A: Campus Bookstore in Garretson (GWP) has rentals. Check syllabus first - many profs use free online materials!

Q: What if I need academic help?

A: Teaching & Learning Center (TLC) in Snoqualmie (SNO) has FREE tutoring! Writing Center helps with papers. Pro tip: Go EARLY in the quarter!

Q: How do I use campus tech?

A: Your UW NetID logs you into everything! Download Microsoft 365 FREE. Campus WiFi: "eduroam". Need help? IT Help Desk in Snoqualmie (SNO) 1st floor.
