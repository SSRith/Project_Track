NEW HUSKY FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q

Where can I get my student ID?

A

Visit the Husky Card Office in Mattress Factory (MAT) with a photo ID!

Q

How do I find my classes?

A

Use our interactive campus map! Buildings use codes (BB = Birmingham Block)

Q

Where's the best coffee on campus?

A

Common Grounds in Dougan (DOU) or Metropolitan Market in Court 17!

Q

How do I get around Tacoma?

A

Free shuttle buses! Pierce Transit routes 400 & 500 stop right on campus.

Q

Where can I study late?

A

Tioga Library (TLB) open until 10PM! Court 17 has 24/7 study lounges.

Q

Where's the best view of Rainier?

A

4th floor of Joy (JOY) or the skybridge in Cherry Parkes (CP)!
